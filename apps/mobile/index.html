  • Tipper

    Description

    Have you ever needed to know the amount for a tip? The server comes, gives you the bill and out that calculator comes. First you try 18% tip, then 19%, 20%.....what if there was an easier way to figure this out? Introducing Tipper! An app dedicated to providing details about your bill.

    Features

    • Quickly find the appropriate tip for your waiter
    • See what your bill total will be like with the tip
    • Let the Emoji’s help you as you select your tip
    • Use the ‘Server Examples’ section to get an idea for what tip your waiter deserves
    • A modern material design theme. No more 19th century look ;)
    • Elegant and effective
